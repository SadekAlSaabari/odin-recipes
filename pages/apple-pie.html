Apple Pie Recipe

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.1 ounce) package pastry for a 9-inch double-crust pie
  • 3/4 cup white sugar, or more for added sweetness
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 6 cups of thinly sliced apples
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients, and preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (or 230 degrees C)

  2. Line a 9-inch pie dish with one pastry crust; set the second one aside.

  3. Combine 3/4 cup of sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Add more sugar if your apples are tart.

  4. Layer apple slices in the prepared pie dish, sprinkling each layer with the cinnamon-sugar mixture.

  5. Dot top layer with small pieces of butter then cover with top crust.

  6. Bake the pie on the lowest rack of the preheated oven for 10 minutes.

  7. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (or 175 degrees C) and continue baking for around 30-35 minutes more until it's golden brown with filling bubbles.

  8. Bon appetit! You can serve it warm or cold, and can add a scoop of ice cream on top if you'd like.
